About

Experienced, registered, and passionate about maths

I'm a licensed Mathematics teacher currently teaching in Canberra and registered with the ACT Teacher Quality Institute (TQI). I've been tutoring since the age of 15 — long before I became a classroom teacher — and have supported more than 200 students through private tutoring, online lessons, and tutoring organisations across Australia and the United States.

My approach focuses on breaking down complex concepts into clear, manageable steps, tailored to each student's individual needs. Whether you're catching up on missed content, preparing for a big exam, or aiming for top results, I provide personalised guidance to help you get there. I'm also happy to support students in building the discipline, resilience, and habits that underpin long-term success.

Beyond mathematics, I'm an endurance athlete who has completed Ironman events and World Major Marathons, and I enjoy scuba diving, table tennis, and travel.
