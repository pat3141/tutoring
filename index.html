About

Teacher & tutor with 15+ years of experience

I am a Mathematics teacher currently teaching in Canberra, registered with the ACT Teacher Quality Institute (TQI). I have been tutoring since the age of 15 and have supported more than 200 students through private tutoring, online lessons, and tutoring organisations across Australia and the United States.

My approach focuses on breaking down complex concepts into clear, manageable steps, tailored to the individual needs of each student. Whether a student needs to catch up on missed content, prepare for an upcoming exam, or aim for top results, I provide personalised guidance to help them get there. I am also happy to support students in building the discipline, resilience, and habits that underpin long-term success.

Outside of teaching, I am an endurance athlete who has completed Ironman events and World Major Marathons. I also enjoy scuba diving, table tennis, and travel.
